Cookie Policy

Introduction

Welcome to our Cookie Policy page. Aviator Gaming Lounge uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your gaming experience. This policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding their usage. By continuing to browse our website, you consent to our use of cookies.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help recognize your browser, remember preferences, and improve website functionality. Cookies do not store personal data but may be linked to other information we collect about you.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Enhance user experience by remembering gaming preferences.
  • Optimize game performance and reduce loading times.
  • Ensure website functionality for interactive gaming features.
  • Analyze gaming activity to improve services and tournaments.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for website functionality, including security, login sessions, and game access.

3.2. Performance Cookies

These cookies help us analyze how players interact with the website, allowing us to improve gaming features and user experience.

3.3. Functional Cookies

These cookies store user preferences, such as game settings, interface themes, and language selections.

3.4. Analytics Cookies

We use analytics tools to track gaming behavior and optimize Aviator Gaming Lounge services.

3.5. Third-Party Cookies

Some content, such as embedded gaming streams or social media sharing options, may set third-party cookies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookie settings in your browser. Disabling cookies may affect certain features, including gaming performance and personalized settings.

5. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in our gaming services. Any updates will be posted on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Cookie Policy, please contact us through the information provided on our website.

Thank you for visiting Aviator Gaming Lounge. We are committed to providing the best gaming experience while ensuring a seamless and secure environment.
