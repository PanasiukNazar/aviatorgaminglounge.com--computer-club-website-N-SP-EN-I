Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to our Privacy Policy page. At Aviator Computer Club, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our gaming club and use our services. By using our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please refrain from using our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Personal Information

When you visit Aviator Computer Club or use our services, we may collect personal information such as:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Preferred gaming setup
  • Membership details

1.2. Non-Personal Information

We also collect non-personal information automatically when you interact with our website or gaming systems, including:

  • IP address
  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • Games played and session duration
  • Referral source

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1. Providing and Improving Services

We use your personal information to:

  • Manage gaming sessions and improve customer experience.
  • Provide membership benefits and gaming discounts.
  • Optimize club performance and gaming station availability.
  • Communicate with you about upcoming gaming events and offers.

2.2. Marketing and Communication

With your consent, we may use your contact details to:

  • Send newsletters and updates about club events.
  • Notify you of exclusive promotions and gaming tournaments.

2.3. Analytics and Research

We use non-personal information to:

  • Analyze gaming trends to improve club services.
  • Enhance gaming setups based on player preferences.

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1. Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted third-party providers who assist us in:

  • Managing club operations and gaming software.
  • Providing secure payment processing for club services.

3.2. Security and Protection

We may disclose your information if necessary to:

  • Ensure fair gaming practices and prevent fraud.
  • Protect club members and our gaming infrastructure.

4. Data Security

We implement various security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive information.
  • Access control for secure login systems.
  • Regular security updates on club devices and networks.

While we take every precaution to secure your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security in an online environment.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1. Access and Updates

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Update your information if it is incorrect or outdated.

5.2. Opting Out

You may opt out of receiving promotional messages by:

  • Unsubscribing from our emails.
  • Contacting us to update your preferences.

5.3. Data Deletion

You may request the removal of your personal data from our systems. We will process your request accordingly.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

6.1. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Improve gaming experience by remembering settings.
  • Analyze website traffic for better services.

You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings.

6.2. Third-Party Tracking

Some of our gaming services may include third-party content that uses tracking technologies. Please review their privacy policies for more details.

7. Gaming Community Guidelines

We encourage a respectful and fair gaming environment. Any violation of our club rules may result in account suspension or removal.

8. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us using the information provided on our website.

Thank you for being part of Aviator Computer Club. We are dedicated to providing a secure and enjoyable gaming experience.
